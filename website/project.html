Smart Orthotics

The first fully-automatic self-adjusting dynamic Orthotics system that adapts to your changing foot structure. Using advanced graphical pressure imagery that shows the patient realtime how his foot is applying pressure, the control system obtains feedback information to make instant adjustments to the shape of the orthotics, ultimately leading to the optimal distribution on the patient's foot.

Advanced medicine requires advanced equipment. That's why Smart Orthotics employs state of the art control systems to adapt the sole to your foot! Unlike traditional orthotics that require continual replacement that can run upwards of $1000/pair (not insured by most insurance companies), this system will keep up with the changing demands of your foot. The result - the last pair of orthotics you will need to purchase!
